Sharjah Fish Resources Authority

Improving the level of services and the effective contribution in a fundamental and pivotal way to sustainable and comprehensive development in all aspects of agriculture and livestock in the Emirate of Sharjah, enhancing the level of well-being of the emirate’s residents and future generations, and contributing to the establishment of a promising future.

Wheat Farm

Wheat Farm in Maliha

The lands of the Emirate of Sharjah have been arable since ancient times, referring to the successful experiences of wheat cultivation in the Maliha, which have proven to be promising experiences that will be supported, developed and taken care of as model farms. We start with you, and with you we turn this empty land into wheat farms. This day is an immortal day that history will remember, because we started on this path.
