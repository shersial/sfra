About the Department

Caring for the development sectors of services, agriculture and livestock affairs and upgrading them is at the heart of the interests of His Highness Sheikh Dr. Sultan bin Muhammad Al Qasimi, Member of the Supreme Council and Ruler of Sharjah - may God protect and preserve him - within the comprehensive strategy drawn up by His Highness to advance community and municipal work and improve its performance levels in all fields. Achieving the desired agricultural and animal development.

Our vision and values

Upgrading the level of agricultural services and effective contribution mainly and pivotally to sustainable and comprehensive development in all aspects of life in the Emirate of Sharjah, enhancing the level of well-being of the emirate's residents and future generations and contributing to establishing a promising future. Providing a level of municipal services efficiently and effectively that contribute to supporting economic, service and cultural development in the Emirate, in accordance with the requirements of sustainable development and adopting best practices with regard to the procedures and systems of municipal work in the Emirate of Sharjah. The department is responsible for supervising, following up, and coordinating work between municipal councils, local and federal government departments and agencies, and public agencies and institutions.
